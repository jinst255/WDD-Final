About Me

I am a passionate, self-taught programmer with over 11 years of experience, beginning at age 9 on calculators and progressing to advanced Python development and AI/ML projects. My drive for efficiency and problem-solving has led me to build automation tools that deliver real business value, including workflow automations and custom AI systems. I thrive in collaborative environments, regularly mentor peers, and have demonstrated leadership as a zone leader during a two-year mission in Argentina, where I also achieved fluency in Spanish.

Currently, I am a Computer Science major at BYU-Idaho with a 4.0 GPA, actively expanding my skills in web development, automation, and machine learning. I am quick to adapt to new languages and frameworks, and I value honest communication and practical solutions. My goal is to build impactful systems with embedded intelligence, contribute to high-performing teams, and continually grow as a developer and leader.
